## Welcome to STEM in Sea Cadets

If you're new to our STEM programs, this guide will help you get oriented and make the most of your training opportunities.

## Step 1: Explore Programs

Visit the [Programs]({{< ref "/programs" >}}) page to see what STEM disciplines are available. Each program page describes what you'll learn and how to get involved.

## Step 2: Find an Event

Check the [Events]({{< ref "/events" >}}) page for upcoming STEM training events. Each event lists a **training code** you'll need for registration.

## Step 3: Register via Quarterdeck

To sign up for a training event:

1. Log in to **Quarterdeck**
2. Search for the event using the **training code** (e.g., "ER-MW-2601")
3. Complete the registration process
4. Confirm with your unit commanding officer

## Step 4: Prepare

Review any prerequisites listed on the event page and come ready to learn. Most STEM programs require no prior experience — just curiosity and a willingness to try.

Visit our [FAQ]({{< ref "/faq" >}}) or [Contact]({{< ref "/contact" >}}) page if you need help.
